FAQS

Frequently Asked

Questions

  • What is Bonk Banner?

    • Bonk Banner is a platform that helps businesses create and manage digital loyalty programs.
    • It provides tools to design personalized rewards, track customer engagement, and analyze performance.
    • This platform is ideal for businesses looking to boost interaction and retention with customized incentives 📈

  • How do I use Bonk Banner?

    • To start using the platform, simply register here and create your first card 📇.
    • Once you finish setting it up, you'll be able to download a PDF with the QR code. Print the PDF or design your own promotional material using the QR code so your customers can scan it.
    • When they scan the code and download their digital card, they’re now part of your loyalty program. You can now use the scanner app to add points or stamps and redeem rewards.
    • You can also follow our step-by-step tutorials here.

  • What types of businesses do they work with?

    • Mainly with food and beverage businesses 🍸🍪🍔🍣
    • However, we can also work with businesses that have recurring customers and require a Loyalty Program, such as: breweries, bars, beauty salons, spas, barbershops, nightclubs, bakeries, retail stores, dry cleaners, hair salons, yoga studios, fitness classes, and more.
    • Ideally, you’re at a point in your business where you're ready to scale and are looking for ways to maintain a constant flow of customers without investing too much in advertising.

  • How long does it take to see results?

    • This varies widely. Some clients see results within a few days of working with us. It also depends a lot on how much you promote the digital cards on your social media and in your business.
    • If there is a strong push for the new digital cards, we hit a Home Run in the first week. If not, it can be difficult for customers to learn about this new and innovative product, and we see fewer downloads.
    • There are many factors at play, but most clients see results within the first week. Once customers KNOW about the digital cards, downloads start coming almost on their own.

  • How much time should I expect to invest in this?

    • Creating a digital card takes less than 15 minutes! And for the customer to download it to their wallet, it takes less than 30 seconds 🤯
    • However, like anything, implementing a new system in your business requires educating both your team and your customers. This can take a few days or weeks, depending on how much emphasis is placed on it.

  • How technical do I need to be?

    • If you know how to use your iPhone or have already downloaded digital passes for flights or movies, you're good to go.
    • Plus, with our tutorials, you don't have to worry about anything 😉.
    • 95% of the technical setup will be handled by our team. We'll just need some simple information from you, like login details, but we’ll take care of the rest.

  • Do my customers need to pay with Apple or Google Pay?

    • No, your customers can pay using any payment method at your establishment. After a successful transaction, the cashier or manager will need to scan the customer's digital card via the scanner app to add the corresponding points or redeem a reward.
