Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: SEPTEMBER 10, 2024

Introduction

This page ("Privacy Policy") outlines our policies and procedures for collecting, using, and disclosing your information, as well as the security measures we have implemented to protect the information you store while using Bonk Banner, LLC. ("Bonk Banner"), including services available through this website, and any other software or service offered by Bonk Banner in connection with such services (the "Services"). By using these Services, you agree to the collection, transfer, processing, storage, disclosure, and other uses of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

What Information Does Bonk Banner Collect and Store?

Personal Information

When you express interest in obtaining more information about the Services or register to use the Services, Bonk Banner asks you to provide your personal contact information, such as your name, business name, address, phone number, and email address (referred to hereafter as your "Personal Contact Information"). When purchasing the Services, Bonk Banner also requires you to provide financial and billing information, such as your billing name and address, credit card number, and the number of employees within your organization who will use the Services ("Billing Information").

Data, Diagnostics & Platform Login Information

With Bonk Banner's services, you can create, upload, store, and share information, such as company descriptions, email IDs, logos, photos, custom emails, and user email IDs (collectively referred to as "Data"). This information is stored and maintained on the Bonk Banner website. If you encounter technical errors while using the Services, Bonk Banner may request your permission to obtain a crash report along with certain system log information documenting the error ("Diagnostic Information"). This information may include your operating system version, hardware, browser version (and .NET version for Windows systems), and your email address if provided. Additionally, certain login information is maintained in a cookie stored locally on your computer (i.e., not on a server) to streamline the login process ("Login Information").

Analytical Information

As you navigate the Bonk Banner website and use our Services, Bonk Banner may also collect information through commonly used data collection tools like cookies and web beacons ("Website Navigation Information").
Website navigation information includes standard details from your web browser (such as browser type and language), your Internet Protocol ("IP") address, and the actions you take on the Bonk Banner website (such as the web pages viewed and links clicked). Collectively, this information is referred to as "Analytics Information."
Third-party providers, including Google, use cookies to display ads based on a user’s previous visits to their website or other websites.
Google’s use of advertising cookies allows them and their partners to display ads to users based on visits to their sites and/or other websites on the internet.
Users can opt-out of personalized ads by visiting Ad Settings or visiting www.aboutads.info

Location Information

Bonk Banner does not collect any real-time geographic location information while using the Services. However, this may occur in the future, and we will ask for your permission before collecting such data.

What Does Bonk Banner Do With the Information It Collects?

Bonk Banner uses the information it collects in the following ways:

  • Personal Contact Information: We primarily use this information to manage your account, provide updates, and send product announcements. As outlined in our Privacy and Terms, we may use some of your information for marketing purposes as described below.
  • Billing Information: Bonk Banner does not store any billing information on its servers. Instead, we use a third-party payment provider, Stripe (www.stripe.com), to store and process all payment-related transactions. Please review Stripe’s privacy policy here.
  • Data, Diagnostic, and Login Information: We use this information only to manage and improve the Services we provide to you.
  • Analytics Information: Bonk Banner may use this information, along with analytics services like Google Analytics, to monitor and analyze usage of the Services, manage the technical aspects of the Services, improve functionality, and verify that users have the necessary permissions to use the Services.

Sharing and Disclosure of Private Information

Third-Party Applications and Their Use

Bonk Banner gives users the ability to link their Data to third-party sites like Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn. Such linking is at the sole discretion of the users. Bonk Banner is not responsible for the linking of user Data to these third-party sites or how these third-party sites use such links.

Marketing and Advertising

As per our Privacy and Terms, you agree to allow Bonk Banner to identify you as a customer and use your name and/or logo on Bonk Banner's website and marketing materials. Sale of Personal Information Bonk Banner does not sell, rent, or trade your private information with third parties in any manner. Service Providers and Business Partners Bonk Banner may use certain trusted third-party companies and individuals to help us provide, analyze, and improve the Services (including, but not limited to, data storage, maintenance services, database management, web analytics, payment processing, and service improvement). These third parties may have access to your information strictly for the purpose of performing these tasks on our behalf and under obligations similar to those in this Privacy Policy.

Non-Private or Non-Personal Information

We may disclose non-private, aggregated, or non-personal information, such as usage statistics of our Services, at our discretion.

Our Use and Disclosure of Your Information

Bonk Banner will not disclose any personally identifiable information about any individual except as described in this Privacy Policy. This applies to both information about our clients and information that our clients provide to us about their customers. We are not restricted in our use of non-personal information that does not directly identify an individual or aggregated, anonymized data (such as the number of clients using our services, geographical distribution of users, the amount of data deleted or stored, etc.).

Internal Use of Your Personally Identifiable Information

We collect, store, and process your personally identifiable information on servers located in the United States. Due to the unpredictable nature of Internet routing, your information may pass through other countries while in transit to our servers. We use the information we collect to:

  • Develop and deliver our services
  • Process your transactions
  • Provide customer service and manage your account
  • Improve our products, services, and marketing

We provide access to personally identifiable information about our users only to those who require it for the above purposes.
Bonk Banner will not sell or rent your personally identifiable information to third parties. We will not share your personally identifiable information with third parties except in the limited circumstances described below:

  • We share information with service providers under contract that assist with our business operations, such as payment processing, fraud investigation, invoice collection, and information management and analysis. These third parties are obligated to protect your information and are prohibited from using your personally identifiable information for any other purpose.
  • We may disclose information we believe, in good faith, is necessary to cooperate in fraud investigations or other illegal activities, investigate violations of our Privacy Policy, or protect our rights, your security, and the safety of others.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

If we decide to make substantial changes to our Privacy Policy, we will notify you by email at the primary email address specified in your account and/or post the changes to this Privacy Policy on the homepage of the website before they take effect. You are responsible for ensuring that we have an active and deliverable email address for you.

We also encourage you to review this Privacy Policy and related documents periodically. We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. No amendment or modification of this Policy will be binding unless in writing and signed by a duly authorized representative of Bonk Banner, or posted on the Site by a duly authorized representative of Bonk Banner.

In the event Bonk Banner undergoes a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, the personally identifiable information of users will, in most cases, be part of the assets transferred. Users will be notified through a prominent notice on the site for 30 days following a change of ownership or control of their personally identifiable information.

Questions, Complaints, and Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@bonkbanner.com
